Anne's Plainfield Country Convinience Store

At Anne's Store you can always find a good cup of coffee, a delicious meal, and friendly conversation.
Our deli is stocked daily with everything from soup to burritos, and we are always prepared to make you a tasty lunch or breakfast sandwich to order. Check out the menus below!
Our store also features a generous seating area complete with free Wi-Fi, a full breakfast menu, an electric vehicle charging station, and an ATM with very low fees. Additionally, we offer catering services.
We look forward to seeing you at Anne's!
We are located at 1190 RT-12A Plainfield, New Hampshire, NH 03781, and are open from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM.
